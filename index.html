Mortality Forecasting for Insurance Risk

A time-series study of the USA–Bulgaria life-expectancy gap for insurance risk modeling. I built life tables from 90 years of Human Mortality Database records, then benchmarked a hybrid Lee–Carter rotation, an LSTM, and a GLM. A reproducible Lee–Carter baseline in R (StMoMo) projects life expectancy to 2050.
